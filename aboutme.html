I'm David Bijl, currently a second year CMD student with a passion for design and web development.

Exploring different forms of creativity has always been a source of joy for me! Since a young age, drawing has been a hobby of mine. As I grew older, I realized I had an affinity for web design, ultimately landing me at CMD.

MY SKILLSET

I'm well versed in a variety of programs and coding languages, with my standout skills being Adobe XD, Figma, Photoshop, Procreate, Premiere Pro, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and TF2 :)
