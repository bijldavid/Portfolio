MY SKILLSET

I'm well versed in a variety of programs and coding languages, with my standout skills being Adobe XD, Figma, Photoshop, Procreate, Premiere Pro, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and TF2 (probably invested the most time here). My general workflow consists of using Adobe XD or Figma for creating designs, Photoshop and Procreate for all graphics related things, and Premiere Pro for video editing. As for coding, I like to dive deep into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. On the off chance I have some spare time, I like to relax by playing some video games - TF2 being my all time favourite!.
